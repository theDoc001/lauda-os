# Principles

Draft. Edit this to reflect your own operating principles. These are the
defaults Lauda OS ships with. Agents respect this file. They will propose
rather than rewrite it.

## Examples to get started

- Validate before building. Time is scarce, not features.
- Propose, never autonomously commit.
- Archive, never delete.
- No em dashes mid-sentence in public writing.
- Flywheels over one-shots.
- Calm over chaos. Stable decisions compound.

Replace with your own. Keep it short. Principles that don't fit on one
screen don't get used.
